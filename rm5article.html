$120 Bezel-Less Phone, Introducing New Xiaomi Redmi 5

Xiaomi, One of the fastest-growing Tech company in China announces its new phone
18:9 Bezel-Less Phone just for $150

Smartphone, these days, are used and loved by everyone in the world.
However, the price of the phones are the only one that is not loved by everyone.

Xiaomi (小米), one of the fastest-growing Tech company in China, announced their 5th Redmi-lineup; and also their new bezel-less 18:9 screen phone.
Mi has been manufactoring one of the greatest phones within an affortable price point, usually half or even 1/3 of the branded phones, such as Samsung or Apple.
Redmi Series, which is their low-end lineups, are the #1 selling phones in India, they sold more than Samsung did last few months.

Redmi lineups were not the "best" choice for $100~$200 phones, even though they had pretty good pricing policy.
However, the camera and display quality made Redmi hard to be used daily, it could definitely be better.
Xiaomi improved their screen qualiity, by adding their first 18:9 bezel-less screen on their phone lineups, and improve camera quality by increasing the size of a camera sensor.
