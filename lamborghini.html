The first Monster-SUV, Lamborghini Urus debuted

Lamborghini, most loved car manufacturer's first World-Class SUV
SUV Platform + Hurucan = Monster SUV "Urus" created

Lamborghini has long held a reputation for creating abstract and flamboyant supercars capable of stunning passers-by wherever they go.
It was therefore a bit of a shock when the supercar manufacturer announced that it was going to introduce a super SUV to its lineup.

Although the Urus concept initially divided opinion, Lamborghini decided to press ahead with a major shakeup of its lineup
and an even bigger change to its manufacturing plant in Santâ€™Agata Bolognese.
Brand new SUV, Lamborghini Urus will be born in this plant.




Wild styling and ferocious performance will combine to help the Urus make its own indelible mark on the SUV world.
With a twin-turbo 4.0-liter V-8 packing 641 hp under its stubby hood, Lamborghini estimates a zero-to-62-mph time of 3.6 seconds and top speed of 190 mph.
An all-wheel-drive system features a locking center diff, while an air suspension keeps the ride civilized.
Enormous carbon-ceramic brakes should provide great stopping power. Look for the Urus in late 2018 priced around $200K.
