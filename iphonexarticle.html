New iPhone X : These will shock you

2017 Untitled Blog Editor's Pick - Brand New iPhone X Hands-On
And the one, final question will answer : Are they worth a money?

"Buying an iPhone X is equivalent to buying high quality coffee", claims Apple's terrible CEO, Tim Cook.
Well, I'm not quite sure, is Tim Cook drinking a coffee blended with a pound of a gold?

Apple announced a new, revolutionary iPhone X back on September 12, 2017 at the Steve Jobs Theater in the Apple Park campus.
The symbol "X", which means 10 in Roman numbers, marks the iPhone's tenth anniversary.
Apple added a bunch of new technology stuff, such as Face ID, 3D Face scanner module, new camera module, 120Hz Retina display and a lot more to say.

Our team decided to skip these boring explanation about these new features added to this phone. Instead, we will give you a direct solution.

Should you buy this phone, or run away from this trash?

Well, our answer is simple : No.

The first reason not to buy iPhone X

"Missing Home Button"



Are you serious, Tim Cook?
Home button was never a chuck of trouble.

Everyone was using Home button every single day; even thousand times a day, more specifically.
Missing home button will take you forever to get used to it. Missing home button is even uncomfortable for early adopters.




The second reason not to buy iPhone X

"Face ID"



Those of you who has a glasses on, good luck.
Once you take off the glasses, iPhone X won't recognize you.

Touch ID did work at any time rapidly. The screen unlocks itself once you put your fingerprint on the home button; it 'was' that simple.
Face ID will ask you to hold your phone straight to your face, then wait for a second. This will be embarrasing, especially with a lot of people.

The last reason not to buy iPhone X

"Price"



You can buy your new iPhone X here.
But, are you REALLY sure you want to buy?

$999. What can you do with this money? You can use this money to :
pay your lease payment on your car for 6 months, eat In-N-Out 200 times, or even buy 3 brand-new Gucci Wallet.
 With three or four new, revolutionary features costs you more? I would not pay this much. Useless.

Now, are you gonna be an idiot, who pays $1000 off their wallet, just to buy this stupid phone?
Or do you want to save that money and use wisely? Well, you got the answer of this question.
